My Projects

In Language Forge, My Projects  is one of the views that people may see if their role permits it. This view shows a list of language projects in rows.

Display this view

  1. Do either of these steps:

    • Click the breadcrumbs link (My Projects).

    • On the browser toolbar, click (My Projects), but do not choose a particular project.

  2. Click (Activity) which is near to the top of the window.

Things you can do in this view

Here are things you may be able to do depending on your role:

  • Click the name of an existing project to display it in Language Forge.

  • Click (Activity) to open the Activity view.

  • Click (Start or Join a New Project).
