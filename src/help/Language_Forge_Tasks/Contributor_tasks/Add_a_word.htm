Add a word

  1. In the Edit Entry view, click (Add Word).

  2. In the right pane, do these steps:

    • Click the Word field and then type the vernacular word that will be a dictionary headword.

    • Click the Meaning field, and then type the meaning of the word.

    • Click the Part of Speech box, and then click the part of speech that corresponds to the meaning of the word.

  3. Do any of these optional steps:

    • Click Add Semantic Domain, and the click the semantic domain that best categorized the meaning of the word.

    • Click Add Example, and then type the an example and translation of that example in the fields that appear.

    • Click Add Meaning to add another meaning to the word. In this case, do step 2 above again.

  4. Click (Save Entry).
